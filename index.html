A Tragicomic Arthouse Short

Solitary Solicitudes (~25 minutes) follows a man’s gray, repetitive life—coffee, oatmeal, odd rituals—unraveling into surreal violence and confession. Shot in Alabama, its stark aesthetic and vivid blood amplify psychological depth.

About the Film

This tragicomic arthouse short blends parody and psychological drama. A man’s monotonous routines in a textureless world spiral into a neighbor’s massacre and a haunting confession. With practical effects (blood rigs, natural light), it’s a striking indie debut targeting Sundance, Sidewalk Film Festival, and MUBI.

About the Filmmaker

A first-time filmmaker and sole proprietor based in Alabama, I’m passionately managing every aspect of Solitary Solicitudes. With meticulous planning and a love for psychological storytelling, I’m bringing raw authenticity to this dark, human tale.
